Google IO 2014
Extended Grand Rapids

Welcome to the Google IO 2014 Extended Event held in Grand Rapids, Michigan!

We are extremely excited to bring this amazing event to you with help from our venue sponsor - GVSU and our other generous sponsors. Come join your local development community to enjoy the live streams from this years Google I/O. We will be offering supplemental live sessions and code labs in addition to Google's content.


We have a large auditorium to view the keynotes and several classrooms for the various breakout sessions. There will be food and drink provided on both days. Stay tuned for schedule information.

Check out the Android application that allows you to view the schedule right from your mobile phone!

Registration for the event is done through Eventbrite and you can register below to join us at this amazing event!
